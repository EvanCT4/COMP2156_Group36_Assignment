George Brown College is a well known public college that was opened in 1968
The college was named after George Brown, a Canadian politician and journalist 

George Brown College or GBC for short, is located in downtown Toronto and has connections to other universities in the local area
examples include Ryerson University who partnered with George Brown College to off collborative programs for students in the field of Nursing and Early Childhood Education.

George Brown features 3 main campus locations: Casa Loma, St. James and the Waterfront campus
