George Brown College (GBC) is a well-known public college of applied arts and technology in Toronto, Ontario. It offers a wide range of diploma, certificate, degree, and postgraduate programs in areas such as business, health sciences, hospitality, construction, technology, and the arts.

Highlights of GBC

Location: Main campuses in downtown Toronto (St. James, Casa Loma, and Waterfront).
Programs: Over 160 full-time programs, including degrees, diplomas, and certificates.
Hands-on Learning: Strong focus on practical, industry-relevant training, with co-op placements and field education opportunities.

Reputation: Well-regarded for its connections with employers, helping students transition into the workforce.
International Students: A popular choice for international students due to its location and strong support services.
